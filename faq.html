FAQ for online booking

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book online?

  • Download Parkaro App. from Android/ iPhone Play Store
  • Sign-up to Parkaro App (Android / iPhone) or Login through Facebook or Google account IDs.
  • You will get OTP on your registered mobile number & enter OTP number on Log in page.
  • Click Nearby Parking and Select a preferred car parking and continue to book.
  • The system will check parking availability for the dates and times specified by you and, if parking is available, will display car park options and prices.
  • Enter your payment details and process the payment
  • Once all the details are correct you will receive a booking confirmation by SMS /generate QR Code.

2. How are parking charges claculated?

Parking charges are calculated on hourly basis, the day and the week based on the times and dates selected when the booking was made.

3. Do I pay for booking fees?

You need to pay for parking charges. No extra booking fees apply.

4. Do Parkapp parking charges including GST?

Yes, the parking charges include GST.
